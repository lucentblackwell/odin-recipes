Kheer (Rice Pudding)

stu spivack, CC BY-SA 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Kheer is a classic Indian dessert, a creamy, sweet rice pudding flavored with cardamom, saffron, and often garnished with nuts and dried fruits.

Ingredients

  • Rice: 1/4 cup Basmati or short-grain rice
  • Milk: 4 cups (full-fat is best)
  • Sugar: 1/2 cup (adjust to taste)
  • Cardamom Powder: 1/2 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 15-20 minutes. Drain before use.

  2. In a heavy-bottomed pan, bring the milk to a boil over medium-high heat.

  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, add the drained rice, and stir well.

  4. Let the kheer simmer gently, stirring frequently (every 5-7 minutes) to prevent the rice from sticking to the bottom.Continue for 40-60 minutes, or until the rice is completely cooked and the milk has thickened and reduced to a creamy consistency.

  5. Add the sugar and cardamom powder. Stir well until the sugar dissolves completely.

  6. Cook for another 5 minutes. Remove from heat and serve warm or chilled.
